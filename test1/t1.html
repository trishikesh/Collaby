Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start a video call or chat with someone on the platform?

To initiate a video call or chat, simply navigate to the user's profile or the team/project space, and click on the video call or chat icon. You can also start a conversation directly from the messaging sidebar.

?

How does version control work on the platform?

Our platform automatically tracks versions of files uploaded to shared projects or repositories. You can view and compare different versions, revert changes if needed, and see who made modifications and when.

?

What features are available for task management?

Our task management tools allow you to create tasks, assign them to team members, set deadlines, and track progress. You can organize tasks into projects, set priorities, and receive notifications for updates and deadlines.

?

Is there a limit to the number of participants in a video call or chat session?

Video calls and chat sessions support multiple participants simultaneously. The exact limit depends on the plan you're subscribed to, with options available for small teams up to larger organizations.
